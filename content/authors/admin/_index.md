I am a lecturer in economics based at the University of Greenwich and affiliated with the Centre of Political Economy, Governance, Finance and Accountability. My research focuses on labour economics, industrial relations and income distribution. At the University of Greenwich, I teach modules in data analysis and  macroeconomics.
